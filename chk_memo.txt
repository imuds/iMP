To check subroutines with separate chk_xx.f90 files, I suggest the following sequence:

1)  chk_therm.f90 	            (caco3 thermodynamic functions)
2)  chk_therm_sb.f90 	        (caco3 thermodynamic subroutines)
3)  chk_grid.f90		        (make sediment grid)
4)  chk_poro.f90 	            (make porosity profile)
5)  chk_coefs.f90 	            (make kinetic and thermodynamic coefficients)
6)  chk_bur.f90                 (calculation of burial velocity)
7)  chk_trans.f90 	            (make transition matrices)
8)  chk_om.f90                  (calculation of om)
9)  chk_om_o2.f90               (calculation of om & o2)
10) chk_cc.f90                  (calculation of cc)
11) chk_clay.f90                (calculation of clay)
12) chk_om_o2_cc_clay.f90       (calculation of om, o2, cc & clay)
13) chk_through.f90             (calculation of om, o2, cc & clay with burial velocity iterated)
